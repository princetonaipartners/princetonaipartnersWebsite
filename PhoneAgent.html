What If The AI Gets Stuck?

We've thought about this. Here's exactly what happens when the AI encounters something it can't handle.

"I want to make sure I understand you correctly. Let me connect you with someone who can help right away."

Transferring with full context...

Graceful Failure, Not Awkward Silence

Our AI is trained to recognize when it's out of its depth. When that happens:

1

Immediate Detection

AI recognizes confusion, repeated questions, or frustrated tone and flags the call immediately.

2

Professional Handoff

Politely tells the caller: "Let me connect you with someone who can help" — no awkward admissions of AI limitations.

3

Context Transfer

You get the full conversation summary, caller info, and reason for transfer—so you're never starting from scratch.

4

Continuous Learning

Every transferred call becomes a training opportunity. We review, improve the AI's knowledge, and it gets smarter.

The Bottom Line: The AI never pretends to know something it doesn't. It's better to transfer gracefully than to give wrong information.

Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about our AI phone receptionist.

What if I already have a phone system?

No problem! We integrate with your existing phone number and system. We simply forward calls to our AI, or we can provide a new number that forwards to you when needed. No hardware changes required.

How long does setup take?

End-to-end setup is completed in under one week. We'll schedule a kickoff call to understand your business, train the AI on your information, and have you live within days.

Which calendar systems work?

We integrate with all major systems: Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly, Acuity Scheduling, and more. If you have a custom system, we can build a custom integration.

Can I review the calls?

Absolutely! You get access to a dashboard with full call transcripts, audio recordings, and analytics. See exactly what was said, how it was handled, and track performance over time.

What if the AI gets confused?

The AI is trained to recognize when it doesn't understand. It will politely say "Let me connect you with someone who can help" and transfer the call to you with full context of the conversation.

Is it customizable?

Completely customizable. Choose the voice personality, greeting scripts, call routing rules, which questions it answers, when to transfer calls, and much more. It's built for YOUR business.

Do I own my data?

Yes, 100%. All call transcripts, recordings, and customer data belong to you. Export everything anytime. Your data, your control.

What about cancellation?

No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee—if you're not satisfied, we'll refund your first month, no questions asked.
