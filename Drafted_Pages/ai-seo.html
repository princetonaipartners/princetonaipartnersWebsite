Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI-driven SEO

How is AI SEO different from traditional SEO?
Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in Google search results. AI SEO optimizes your business to be recommended by AI assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews. These AIs look for structured data (schema markup), reviews, website speed, and clear content—not just keywords and backlinks.
How long does it take to see results?
Unlike traditional SEO which takes 6-12 months, AI SEO can show results in 2-4 weeks. Once we implement schema markup and optimize your Google Business Profile, AI assistants can immediately start understanding and recommending your business. Full optimization typically takes 2-3 months.
Do I need a new website for AI SEO?
No! We can add AI SEO to any existing website. We'll implement schema markup, optimize your content structure, and ensure your site is fast and mobile-friendly. If your current site is very outdated, we may recommend upgrading, but it's not required to get started with AI SEO.
What's schema markup and why does it matter?
Schema markup is code that tells AI assistants exactly what your business does. It includes your hours, services, prices, reviews, and more in a format AIs can easily read. Without schema, AIs have to guess what your business offers—and they often get it wrong or skip you entirely. With schema, you're giving AIs the exact information they need to confidently recommend you.
Will this help my Google rankings too?
Yes! Everything we do for AI SEO also helps traditional Google rankings. Schema markup, fast websites, great reviews, and quality content are ranking factors for both AI assistants AND Google search. You get the best of both worlds.
Can I test if my business is getting recommended now?
Absolutely! Go to ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity and ask: "Find me a [your business type] in [your city]." See if you show up in the recommendations. Then look at what information the AI provides about you—is it accurate? Complete? That's a good indicator of how well optimized you are for AI.
