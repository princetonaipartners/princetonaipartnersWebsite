Smart Document Processing (RAG)

Imagine an employee who has instantly memorized every document, manual, and past project in your company's history. Our Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) agents act as a super-intelligent internal search engine, providing instant, accurate answers to complex staff questions by drawing directly from your own private data.

  • Eliminates time wasted searching for information.
  • Ensures consistent and accurate answers for training and operations.
  • Keeps your proprietary data secure and private.
What is RAG? Retrieval-Augmented Generation allows an AI to "look up" information in your private documents before answering a question. This prevents the AI from making things up and ensures its answers are factual and highly relevant to your specific business.
