# Princeton AI - Brand Vision & Positioning

## Who We Are

**Princeton AI is a custom product studio** that builds bespoke technology solutions for businesses of all sizes. We're full-stack developers who bring AI innovation to every project where it makes sense.

## What We Do

We build **anything custom**:
- Web Applications (Next.js, React, full-stack)
- Mobile Apps (iOS, Android, React Native)
- Custom Bots (Telegram, WhatsApp, Discord)
- AI Integration (RAG agents, AI phone systems, chatbots)
- Process Automation (n8n, custom workflows, APIs)
- Web Scraping & Data Extraction
- Cloud Infrastructure & DevOps
- Bespoke Software Solutions

## Our Competitive Edge

**AI-enhanced development capability.** While we're full-stack developers first, our ability to seamlessly integrate AI into any solution sets us apart. We can:
- Add RAG systems to existing applications
- Build AI-powered phone systems from scratch
- Create intelligent bots that understand context
- Automate workflows with AI-driven decision making
- Extract and structure data with AI precision
