OUR STORY

Where Tradition Meets Innovation

At Maison Laurent, we celebrate the rich heritage of French gastronomy while embracing contemporary culinary techniques. Each dish is thoughtfully crafted using the finest seasonal ingredients sourced from local farms and artisan producers.

Chef Laurent Beaumont brings over 20 years of Michelin-starred experience to create an unforgettable dining journey that honors tradition while pushing creative boundaries.
